Job Offer Letter by Email

This module will send Job Offer letter to the selection person


Overview

HR departments need not worry about job offer letter for your selection person. In this module we have included the feature to send an email to the selection person.

Features:

  • Adds a cron job to invoke email
  • Adds an email template


Go to: Recruitment/Applications/All Applications

  • Enter Subject / Application Name
  • Enter Applicant's Name
  • Enter Email
